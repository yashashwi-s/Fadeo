## Description

Your Mac plays the wrong sound for what you are doing, and you fix it by hand all day: start the noise, pause it for a call, switch it when you change tasks. I built **Fadeo** to do that for you.

Fadeo is a free, open-source menu bar app that plays, pauses, and switches your audio based on context: the frontmost app, the desktop Space you are on, whether your camera or mic is live (so meetings pause your audio automatically), your Focus mode, and the time of day. A Now screen shows you exactly why it made each decision, so it never feels like a black box.

For sound it either synthesizes ambient textures on device (brown, pink, white noise, rain, ocean, wind, fan, with no audio files shipped) or conducts your Spotify or Apple Music, including a specific playlist. It is event driven, so it stays near 0% CPU at idle, and it never touches your system volume, so each workspace keeps its own level while your volume keys stay yours.
